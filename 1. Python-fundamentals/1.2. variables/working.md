## working with variables

```
>>> add = 10 + 10
>>> sub = 20 - 10
>>> multi = 5 * 5
>>> div = 10 / 5
>>>
>>> print(add, sub, multi, div)
20 10 25 2

>>> print(add, sub, multi, div)
20 10 25 2
>>> result = (add * sub) - (multi * div)		# (20 * 10) - (25 * 2)
>>> print('Result: ', result)
Result:  150
```
